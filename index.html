About GREEN BUILDING


As a responsive client-oriented firm, GREEN BUILDING Architects & engineers continue to excel in creating innovative architecture that is fresh and inspirational in both form and function. Over the course of the last three decades, the work of GREEN BUILDING Architects & Engineers has earned the firm recognition as one of the most respected architectural design firms in the region. The firm has been the recipient of numerous awards recognizing the wide range of design and technical accomplishments.

Inventive design and superior execution are the hallmarks of what we do. Our design approach is methodical and pragmatic yet innovative and rich. We deploy extensive construction knowledge and profound attention to detail from the conceptual development of a project through execution and handover. At every level, we exceed our client’s expectations with unmatched quality and style.

Our Services


GREEN BUILDING offers a full range of services providing you with a one stop turnkey solution for your development’s design requirements. Our services include master planning, interior design, engineering, architecture, site supervision and project management.
